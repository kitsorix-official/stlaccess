Privacy Policy

I believe your scale data should stay exactly where it belongs: with you. Here is how I handle your information.

No Accounts

I don't ask for your name, email, or any personal details.

Local Storage

Your "My Library" data is stored in your own browser's memory.

Consent Only

Analytics are only enabled if you explicitly click "Accept All".

1. Data We Collect

STLAccess does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). When you use our calculator, the mathematical operations happen locally on your device.

Usage Data: If you grant consent via our cookie banner, I use Google Analytics to understand how many people use my tools and which features are most popular. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

2. Cookies and Storage

I use two types of storage technologies:

  • LocalStorage: This is used to save your conversions in the "My Library" feature. This data never leaves your computer and is not accessible by me.
  • Cookies: If accepted, I use small text files for Google Analytics (GA4) to track site traffic trends. These are managed according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Your Rights

Since I don't store your data on my servers, you have total control:

  • Access & Portability: You can see all your saved data in the "My Library" tab.
  • Deletion: You can delete your entire library at any time by clicking "Clear All" in the Library tab or by clearing your browser's site data.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can change your cookie preferences by clicking the "Privacy Preferences" banner.

4. Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For traffic analysis (subject to consent).
  • Cloudflare: For secure hosting and DDoS protection.

5. Contact & Legal

If you have questions about this policy or my privacy practices, please contact me via my GitHub repository. For information on service usage, see my Terms of Service.
