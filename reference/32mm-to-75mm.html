32mm to 75mm
Conversion Guide

234.4%

The Mathematics

Converting from 32mm Heroic to 75mm Display Scale requires a massive increase to approximately 234.4%. This is perfect for painters who want to take their favorite tabletop characters and turn them into display-worthy large-format projects.

// Conversion Formula
(75 / 32) * 100 = 234.375% (Standard: 234.4%)

Physical Height Check

32mm Model 32.0mm
75mm Model 75.0mm

3D Printing Considerations

Uniform Scaling

Massive upscaling requires absolute precision. Ensure **Uniform Scaling** is active to avoid any stretching that would be very obvious at this size.

Re-Supporting

CRITICAL: Pre-supported files designed for 32mm will almost certainly fail at 234%. You must re-support the model using heavy/large supports to handle the increased weight and suction forces.

Hollowing

At 75mm, solid resin prints become very heavy and expensive. We highly recommend hollowing the model and adding drainage holes to save material and reduce print failure risk.
