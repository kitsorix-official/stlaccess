The Mathematics

Converting from 35mm (Legion Scale) to 32mm (Heroic Scale) requires a reduction to approximately 91.4%. This is ideal for players wanting to mix Star Wars Legion sculpts with Warhammer or other 32mm heroic systems.

// Conversion Formula
(32 / 35) * 100 = 91.42% (Standard: 91.4%)

35mm Model 35.0mm

3D Printing Considerations

Downscaling

When downscaling, details become finer and thinner. Ensure your resin and printer can handle the slightly higher level of detail required for 91% scale.

Style Matching

Legion models (35mm) are often "True Scale," while many 32mm systems are "Heroic." Even at 91%, the Legion models may look thinner or more realistically proportioned than their heroic counterparts.

Base Sizes

Legion uses 27mm bases, while 32mm systems often use 28.5mm or 32mm. You may need to print new bases or use adapters after scaling the miniature.
