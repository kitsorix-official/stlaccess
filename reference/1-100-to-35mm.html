1:100 to 35mm

Converting 15mm scale (1:100) models for use in 35mm boutique systems.

Recommended Scale

200.0%
Open in Engine

Mathematical Proof

The conversion from 1:100 to 35mm is a straight doubling of size. 1:100 scale (often called 15mm) has a standard eye-level height of 17.5mm for a 1.75m human.

(35.0mm / 17.5mm) * 100 = 200.0%

Scale Baselines

  • Source (1:100) 17.5mm Eye
  • Target (35mm) 35.0mm Eye
  • Multiplier 2.0x

Scaling Considerations

When scaling 15mm (1:100) models up by 200%, keep the following in mind:

  • Detail Density: 15mm models are often sculpted with exaggerated features for castability at small sizes. At 200%, these features will look very chunky.
  • Wall Thickness: Hollowed models designed for 15mm will have very thick walls at 35mm. You may want to re-hollow the model to save resin.
  • Base Sizes: 1:100 models usually have integral bases. You will likely need to clip these off and use standard 35mm or 40mm bases.
