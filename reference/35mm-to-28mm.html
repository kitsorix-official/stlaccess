35mm to 28mm
Conversion Guide

Recommended Slicer Percentage
80.0%
Open in Engine

The Mathematics

Converting from 35mm (Large Scale) to 28mm (True Scale) requires a reduction to exactly 80.0%. This is perfect for fitting Legion models or MCP terrain into a 28mm skirmish game or RPG.

// Conversion Formula
(28 / 35) * 100 = 80.0%

Physical Height Check

35mm Model 35.0mm
28mm Model 28.0mm

3D Printing Considerations

Significant Downscaling

At 80%, small details like thin swords or antenna might become extremely brittle. Handle with care after curing.

True Scale Matching

35mm Legion minis are often very realistically proportioned. At 80%, they fit perfectly with "True Scale" 28mm historicals, but may look tiny next to "Heroic" 28mm minis.

Resin Choice

Consider using a tough or ABS-like resin for 80% downscales to ensure the thinner parts survive handling during gaming sessions.
