1:72 to 35mm
Conversion Guide

Recommended Slicer Percentage
144.0%
Open in Engine

The Mathematics

Converting from 1:72 (Small Scale) to 35mm (Legion Scale) requires a significant increase to 144.0%. This is common for players wanting to use 1:72 vehicles or aircraft as background terrain or objective markers in a 35mm game.

// Conversion Formula
(35 / 24.3) * 100 = 144.03% (Standard: 144.0%)

Physical Height Check

1:72 Model 24.3mm
35mm Model 35.0mm

3D Printing Considerations

Significant Upscaling

At 144%, the original 1:72 model will be nearly 50% larger. Ensure the source file has enough polygon density to avoid "faceted" surfaces on curves.

True Scale vs Legion

1:72 models are usually "True Scale," while Legion is "Slightly Heroic." Even at 144%, the 1:72 models will look more realistically proportioned and potentially thinner than official Legion sculpts.

Hollowing

For large 1:72 vehicles upscaled to 144%, hollowing the model is highly recommended to save resin and prevent suction issues during the printing process.
