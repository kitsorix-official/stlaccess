1:48 to 32mm
Conversion Guide

Recommended Slicer Percentage
87.8%
Open in Engine

The Mathematics

Converting from 1:48 (Quarter Scale / O-Scale) to 32mm (Heroic Scale) requires a reduction to approximately 87.8%. This is common for gamers using 1:48 aircraft or terrain in a 32mm sci-fi setting.

// Conversion Formula
(32 / 36.45) * 100 = 87.79% (Standard: 87.8%)

Physical Height Check

1:48 Model 36.45mm
32mm Model 32.0mm

3D Printing Considerations

Downscaling

At 88%, the model becomes slightly smaller. Ensure your slicer settings are optimized for fine details, as thin wings or landing gear will become more delicate.

Scale Perception

1:48 vehicles often look "right" next to 32mm minis without any scaling, but mathematically, the infantry should be slightly smaller. Using 88% creates a more grounded, realistic look.

Terrain Synergy

1:48 architectural models (O-Scale) are fantastic for 32mm gaming. Scaling them to 88% ensures doors and windows align perfectly with 32mm base heights.
