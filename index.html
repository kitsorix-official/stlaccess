Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common scaling questions from the 3D printing community.

What scale is Warhammer 40k miniatures?

Official Games Workshop miniatures use 32mm heroic scale (measured from eye level at ~160mm). Convert from true 28mm by scaling your STL to 114.3% in your slicer.

How do I convert 28mm to 32mm for D&D?

Multiply your 28mm STL file by 114.3% to achieve heroic 32mm scale. This accounts for the standard 175cm human baseline and eye-level measurement methodology used by STLAccess.

Does resin shrinkage affect miniature scale?

Yes. Standard resin shrinks ~1-2% during curing. For critical scale accuracy, add +1.5% compensation to your slicer scale (e.g., use 115.8% instead of 114.3% for 28mm→32mm).
